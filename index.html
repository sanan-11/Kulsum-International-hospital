ABOUT KULSUM INTERNATIONAL

A Legacy of Advanced Medical Care

Kulsum International Hospital is a state-of-the-art healthcare provider established in 2011 by the Saif Group. Located at the prime business hub (Blue Area) of Islamabad, we are dedicated to providing medical treatments that align with international benchmarks.

Our commitment to quality, patient safety, and ethics makes us the preferred choice for patients in Northern Pakistan. We continuously invest in advanced diagnostics, robotic-assisted surgical fields, and modern healing wards.
